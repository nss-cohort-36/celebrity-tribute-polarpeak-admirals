Personal Information

The Birth of a Starr

Ringo Starr was born Richard Starkey in Dingle, an inner city area of Liverpool, England on July 7, 1940.


Ob-La-Di, Ob-La-Da, Life Goes On


Starr currently lives with his wife, Barbara Bach, in Beverly Hills, California.


All You Need is Love

Ringo Starr married Maureen Cox in 1965 but the marriage ended in divorce in 1975. During filming of the movie Caveman in 1980, Ringo met Barbara Bach and the pair were wed on April 27, 1981.
