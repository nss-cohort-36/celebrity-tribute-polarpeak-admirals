Who Is Ringo?

Ringo Starr was born Richard Starkey on July 7, 1940 in Liverpool, England.

He is most famous for being the drummer and goofiest goober of legendary pop-rock group, The Beatles.

He is also known as:

  • "The Funny Beatle"
  • "The Funny-Looking Beatle"
  • "That Guy Who Can't Drum"

He currently resides in Beverly Hills, California, which may explain why he hasn't been seen without sunglasses on in decades.
